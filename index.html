Frequently Asked Questions

What is refresh rate?

Refresh rate, measured in Hertz (Hz), is the number of times your display updates per second. A higher refresh rate results in smoother motion and better responsiveness.

How accurate is this tool?

This tool uses multiple detection methods and statistical analysis to provide highly accurate measurements of your display's performance characteristics.

What is frame time?

Frame time is the duration between each frame update, measured in milliseconds. Lower frame times indicate better performance and smoother animations.
